NutriFeed

An intelligent baby feeding solution that tracks nutrition intake, monitors temperature, and provides insights for optimal infant care.

Explore more

About

NutriFeed is an innovative smart feeding bottle designed to help mothers monitor and manage their baby's nutrition with ease and confidence. Equipped with advanced sensors and a connected mobile app, NutriFeed automatically measures milk quantity, tracks feeding duration, and monitors temperature in real time to ensure every feed is safe, healthy, and perfectly timed.

Its built-in alerts notify you when the milk is reaching unsafe temperatures or when it's time for the next feed, making caregiving effortless. With a hygienic, BPA-free design and smart sterilization reminders, NutriFeed combines modern technology with everyday practicality, giving parents the peace of mind they deserve. Whether at home or on the go, NutriFeed ensures smarter feeding, healthier habits, and happier babies.

Today, NutriFeed stands as a symbol of how ADI and IoT can blend to deliver meaningful, life-enhancing solutions for parents, infants, and caregivers.

Purpose

To develop a smart feeding bottle that monitors baby feeding patterns and provides personalized recommendations to parents.

Features
  • Track feeding habits
  • Milk consumption estimation
  • Temperature monitoring
  • Sterilization reminders
  • Data sharing with caregivers
Tech Used
  • ESP32 Microcontroller
  • Temperature & Weight Sensors
  • Cloud Integration
  • Mobile App
